1. Floods

Punjab faces floods mainly due to overflowing of rivers like Sutlej and Beas during heavy monsoons. Floods damage crops, homes, and cause displacement of people.
Survival Tip: Move to higher ground, avoid walking in flood water, and store emergency supplies like food and clean water.

2. Drought / Water Scarcity

Excessive groundwater usage and low rainfall create water scarcity in Punjab. Farmers are worst affected as crops fail.
Survival Tip: Store water wisely, practice rainwater harvesting, and grow less water-intensive crops.

3. Heatwaves

During peak summer, temperatures in Punjab often cross 45¬∞C, leading to heat strokes and dehydration.
Survival Tip: Stay indoors in the afternoon, drink plenty of water, and wear light cotton clothes.

4. Air Pollution & Smog

Smog in Punjab mainly occurs due to stubble burning in October-November. It causes respiratory diseases and reduces visibility.
Survival Tip: Wear N95 masks, use air purifiers, and avoid outdoor activities during high smog days.

5. Industrial Accidents

Industrial hubs like Ludhiana often see chemical leaks, factory fires, and explosions. Workers and nearby residents are at high risk.
Survival Tip: Follow safety drills, stay away from accident zones, and call emergency services quickly.

6. Epidemics / Diseases

Dengue, malaria, and cholera spread during monsoon due to stagnant water and poor sanitation.
Survival Tip: Keep surroundings clean, use mosquito nets, and drink boiled water.

7. Road & Rail Accidents

Punjab faces frequent road and railway accidents due to dense transport networks and reckless driving.
Survival Tip: Follow traffic rules, avoid drunk driving, and use railway crossings safely.

8. Building Collapse / Urban Fire


Poor safety regulations and illegal construction cause building collapses and urban fires.
Survival Tip: Use fire exits, avoid lifts during fire, and call emergency numbers immediately.

9. Locust Attacks


Locusts entering from Rajasthan sometimes destroy large areas of Punjab‚Äôs crops.
Survival Tip: Use pest control sprays, coordinated spraying by farmers, and government monitoring systems.

10. Earthquake

Punjab is in a moderate seismic zone, so earthquakes can occur, especially near the Himachal border. Survival Tip: Drop, cover, and hold under strong furniture; stay away from windows and tall objects.
